01 — context

What I Never
Properly Explained

At first, talking to you felt easy.
We laughed, joked, and shared conversations that genuinely made my day better.

I know I flirted a lot, probably more than I should have.
And looking back, I understand how my actions may have created expectations that I wasn't ready to fulfill.

The truth is that I was never really prepared for a relationship.
Not because of you.
Not because you did something wrong.

I was dealing with my own situations, responsibilities, confusion, and a schedule that often pulled me away from people who mattered.

None of that excuses my behavior, though.

02 — apology

I'm Sorry

  • I'm sorry for disappearing.
  • I'm sorry for replying late.
  • I'm sorry for making it seem like I didn't care.

The reality is that I often got caught up in everything happening around me and failed to communicate properly.

You deserved honesty.
You deserved clarity.

And instead of explaining things, I became distant.
That wasn't fair to you.

03 — what matters

What I Want You
To Know

I don't want this page to make you feel guilty.
I don't want sympathy.

I simply want you to know that my distance was never because you weren't enough.

You were kind. You were genuine.
You treated me better than I probably deserved.

My absence came from my own circumstances and mistakes, not because of your value.
